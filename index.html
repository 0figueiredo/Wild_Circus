ABOUT US

WildCircus is a nonprofit arts and education organization based in Reims, France. Our mission is to promote the skills, culture and traditions of circus, and to inspire youth to engage in life-changing adventures in the circus arts.

Wild circus is the brain child of Founding Director Clement Bechetoille , who ran off to Europe to apprentice himself in circus life. It was there that he learned to pound stakes, put up the Big Top, work with animals and train with circus artists. Clement spent more than a decade performing as a circus manager in several circuses in Great Britain, Sweden or in Denmark. He taught mime, movement, and circus skills for many years in schools and colleges in France and abroad. Clement founded in 2016 Wild Circus on a 38 acre parcel nestled among the rolling hills of Verzy.
